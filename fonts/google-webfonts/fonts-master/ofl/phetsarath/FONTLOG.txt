Phetsarath fonts and any derivative works. 


Basic Font Information

Phetsarath is a font for the Laotian language of Laos. 
It was commmissioned by the Ministry of Posts and 
Telecommunications of the Laos government.
  
More specifically, this release supports the following 
Unicode ranges: Lao

There are no source files.

To contribute to the project contact Danh Hong.
