Kdam Thmor is a display face based on the writing style of a brush used on a wall. It is designed for Khmer Unicode text. Kdam Thmor has an edgy style, a medium size and is suitable for headings and large typography. It was designed in 2013 by Tep Sovichet, a high school student from Cambodia, who learned typeface design with local expert Danh Hong. It is his second font published in Google Fonts.
