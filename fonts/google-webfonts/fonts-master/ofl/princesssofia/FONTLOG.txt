Basic Font Information

She's a real princess who rules the casa with love, grace and fancy penmanship.  A casual italic calligraphy inspired style perfect for titling with a little flair.

Designed by Crystal Kluge of Tart Workshop (a DBA of Font Diner, Inc)

Princess Sofia is a Unicode typeface family that supports 
languages that use the Latin script and its variants, and 
could be expanded to support other scripts.

More specifically, this release supports the following Unicode
ranges: Latin-1

To contribute to the project contact the Font Diner at 
support@fontdiner.com

There is one .vfb Source file:

1. PrincessSofia-Regular-TTF.vfb TrueType outlines with hinting 
   adjustments corresponding to the TTF file
