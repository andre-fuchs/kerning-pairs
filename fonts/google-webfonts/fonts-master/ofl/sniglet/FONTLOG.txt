FONTLOG for the Sniglet fonts

This file provides detailed information on the Sniglet Font Software.

This information should be distributed along with the Sniglet fonts and any derivative works.

Basic Font Information:

A rounded display face that is great for headlines.

Originally designed in 2008 by Haley Fiege in Extra-bold.

In 2013 Brenda Gallo and Pablo Impallari added a Regular weight

To contribute to the project contact Haley Fiege (haley@kingdomofawesome.com) and Pablo Impallari (impallari@gmail.com)
