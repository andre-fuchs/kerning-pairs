FONTLOG for the Fenix font

This file provides detailed information on the Fenix font Software.

This information should be distributed along with the Fenix fonts and any
derivative works.

Basic Font Information

Fenix was designed by Fernando Díaz.

Fenix is a serif typeface designed for display and long texts, its foundations
are based in calligraphy, with strong serifs and rough strokes. Its
proportions seek to gain space in height and width. Fénix is elegant at large
sizes and legible at the same time, with a lot of rhythm in small sizes.

Fenix is a Unicode typeface that supports languages that use the Latin script
and its variants, and could be expanded to support other scripts.

More specifically, this release supports the following Unicode ranges: Latin-1

To contribute to the project contact Fernando Díaz at fenix@ferfolio.com
