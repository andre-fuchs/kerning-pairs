Sunndal Rockefestival var en musikkfestival arrangert av frivillige på Sunndalsøra på Nordmøre i Møre og Romsdal. Festivalen ble arrangert i perioden 1998 til 2006.
2006: Åge Aleksandersen og Sambandet, Raga Rockers, Swerve, Backstreet Girls, Bad Apples, Dynamite, Amulet, Brut Boogaloo,Josephine, The Halsa Hazel Band, Toys And Flavors, No Class, Diamondog, Defective, Thunderbolt.
2005: Flare, Dynamite, Emmerhoff and the melancholy babies, Griffin, Winston, teXum, No Class, Sissy Wish, Crest of Darkness.
2004: Bad Obsession, Peeker, No Class, Griffin, Velvet Goldmine, Dynamite, Ribozyme, Autotune, JohnDoe, Valhalla, Mayers Bluesband, Winston, Delaware, Silence the Foe, Nullskattesnylterne, Tømmermenn, Thunderbolt.
2003: Pond, Theo, Speedqueen Superstars, Godsize, Agathon's Favorite, Deadbeats (S), Das Neue Konzept, Black Oblivion, Autotune, Thulsa Doom, Lumsk, Schmucks.
2002: Volt, Fortytwo, Void, Silver, Velvet Goldmine, Pond, Theo, Action Pilots, Wærstinga`n, Pistolero, Brasco, Chime Poppers, Indira, Popium, El Rodeo, Skidmark, Electric Ladyland.
2001: Homo Lupus, Action Pilots, Numskull, Soundscape Silence, Hellride, Spon, Bomber, Shotgun Bluez (S), Theo, Jamesband, Das Neue Konzept, Thulsa Doom, Peeker.
2000: Shotgon Bluez (S), Velvet Goldmine, Peeker, Onematopoeia, Sentinel, Tömmermenn, Wærstinga`n, Dewotine, Skavank, Planet Trash (S), Billion Dollar Mission, Soundscape Silence, Sniffing Glue (S), Boy.
1999: Quadrophonia, Shotgun Bluez (S), Lash Out, Slagmark, Wretched, Scrambled Eggs, Borkum Riff, Rock Minitor, Sniffing Glue (S), Sissy Dogfish, Bigbang, Subway Cirkus, Children of The Revolution.
